Eth1Data

A vote on recent data the Eth1 chain.

  • depositRoot

    The SSZ List hash_tree_root of the deposits in the deposit contract.

  • depositCount

    The number of deposits that have occured thusfar.

  • blockHash

    The eth1 block hash that contained the deposit_root. This block_hash might be used for finalization of the Eth1 chain in the future (similar to how the FFG contract was going to be used to finalize the eth1 chain).
