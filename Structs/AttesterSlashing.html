AttesterSlashing

Beacon attesters can be slashed if they sign two conflicting attestations where conflicting is defined by is_slashable_attestation_data which checks for the Casper FFG “double” and “surround” vote conditions.

  • attestation1

    The first of the two slashable attestations. Note that this is in “indexed” form.

  • attestation2

    The first of the two slashable attestations. Note that this is in “indexed” form.
